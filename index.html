US PLASTIC WASTE EXPORT


OUT OF SIGHT OUT OF MIND?


United States is the top exporter of Plastic Waste worldwide, sending over a MILLION tons of Plastic waste overseas in 2018. Majority of this waste goes to developing countries, who don't have the resources to properly manage the waste, and thus, a huge propotion of this waste ends up in landfills and our oceans.
